Z Carpentry Inc.


  We are a NY-based company focused on designing, building, and installing your cabinetry and millwork needs. Whether you are a home-owner, business-owner, designer, or contractor, we make sure that you receive a high quality product in a timely manner at a competitive price.

For over 20 years, Z Carpentry Inc. has been building and remodeling homes and businesses to their customers’ tastes and desires. We nurture this aim by being the utmost in craftsmanship and service, thanks to the veteran staff working in our office, at our mill shop, and on site. Our staff includes office personnel, carpenters, wood-workers, finishers, designers, and supervisors, ready to give your project their full attention every step of the way.
