Michigan Installation and Trim


You may not have a vintage home or an architectural pedigree, but giving your home a touch of history - inside and out - with architectural embellishments of a period style can be as easy as calling.
We offer a complete interior or exterior package
in carpentry, cabinetry and architectural trims.
A strong interest in furnituremaking, carving and traditional design has blended the craft of carpentry into a way of life.
Whether remodeling or building new elements such as columns and architectural trims can be added to embellish a home's exterior, add to its value, and provide the look and feel of a period home. All the embellishments you need to transform a timid tract house, a ranch, or build the vintage home are within the skills and craft of Michigan Installation and Trim.
