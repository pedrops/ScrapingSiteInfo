Jordan Woodworking, LLC


Wood has an inherent warmth that other materials simply don’t possess. Wood brings functional beauty into our lives, and when we design our furniture and interior spaces with thoughtful, custom woodworking, we create a place that is more than just a shelter from the elements — we create a home. At Jordan Woodworking, we create beautiful homes and offices in Colorado with a genuine passion for wood and the people who use and live with it. From design to installation and finishing, we craft each piece with a level of creativity, precision, and attention to detail that seeks to define excellence. We build with one purpose: to combine the most excellent service with craftsmanship that integrates beauty, function and durability.
