All Furniture Services LLC


We offer best prices and guarantee our work by manufactory trained professionals!
We offer Same Day, 24/7 services for emergency: Take Apart, Break Down, Dismantling, Assembling and Disassembling for: Sofa, Sofa Bed, Sleeper, couch, Loveseat, Chairs, Ottoman, Sectional, Chaise, Murphy Bed, Daybed, Wall Unit, Entertainment Centers, Armoire, Bed, Office Furniture (partition, cubicles, desks)...
We are a professional furniture service company that offers repairs, restoration, estimates, adjustments, reports, inspections, cleaning, etc. In some cases we can determine even the age of the damaged area as well as if they were previously repaired, if there was any recalls or manufactory defects on the claimed damaged items. We also provide weight and pictures, if needed.

Some of the repair services we offer are:
Furniture: Repair, Restoration, Assembly, Disassembly, Touch-up, Polishing, Leather Repair & Dyeing, Refinishing, Cleaning, Upholstery, Recliner & Sleeper Mechanisms, Adjustments, etc.
Rug, Carpet: Cleaning, Restoration and Repair.
Hardwood floor & wall panels: Repair, Restoration, Touch-up, Refinishing, and polishing of small damaged areas.
Construction: Repair, Repainting, etc.
Metal: Welding and Iron work, Polishing and recoating.
Antique and ceramic: Repair, Restoration, Color matching, etc.
We work 7 days a week including evenings.
Service areas are in New York, New Jersey, Connecticut and Long Island.
We work with numerous moving, insurance, furniture, construction and warranty companies. Some of them can be found at our web site www.furnitureservices.com
If you need references or feedbacks, we can provide as many as you need!
