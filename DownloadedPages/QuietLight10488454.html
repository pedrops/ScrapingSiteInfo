Asking Price: $1,250,000 + Inventory

Reading Time: < 1 minute

Started in 2009, this 11-year-old Amazon FBM and FBA, private label business has enjoyed sustained success over the years. With 630 active SKUs, the business sells high-margin, branded tools for automotive mechanics for engine and chassis repairs. They are also a distributor for flexible cylinder hones for a domestic supplier and a drop shipper of vinyl tire covers. This business has a long-standing relationship with an overseas buyer representative, which gives them the ability to source any tool on the market. Because of this, they also offer tools outside of the automotive segment and have unlimited potential to add new SKUs to their brand.

The business enjoys multiple revenue channels, with Amazon accounting for 60% (90% FBM, 10% FBA), eBay 30%, wholesale 5%, and their website 5%. With more than 11 years since launching their first SKU, the brand has established itself deeply within the Amazon marketplace, boasting over 500 reviews for the main product, maintaining the Amazon Choice badge on several products, and earning an average star rating of 4.2. All products are sourced in China with reliable and proven vendors and all FBM is handled via a 3PL in the USA.

The owner believes there are several obvious areas for growth, including increasing SKUs to FBA, expanding to Walmart.com, and upgrading to Shopify from his current Yahoo stores. The owner is selling because he would like to retire and is fully prepared to train and transition a new owner.
